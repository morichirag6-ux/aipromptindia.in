What is the Daily AI Sales Missions?

Daily AI Sales Missions gives you one focused AI sales challenge every day for 30 days. Each mission is a specific, actionable task — a prompt to run, a message to write, a skill to practice — that builds your AI-powered sales habit one day at a time.

How to use it — 4 steps
1
Open the missions page
See today's mission at the top.
2
Read the mission brief
Each mission takes 10–20 minutes and has a clear objective.
3
Complete using AI
Use the provided prompt with ChatGPT or Gemini to complete the mission.
4
Track your streak
Come back tomorrow to keep your 30-day streak going.
Who is this for?

Salespeople who know they should be using AI but haven't built the habit yet, SDRs looking for structured skill-building, and sales managers who want a simple daily training activity for their team.

Why use an AI prompt instead of doing it manually?

Most salespeople try AI once, get an average result, and give up. The 30-day mission structure is designed to build competence gradually — starting with simple tasks and progressing to advanced workflows. By Day 30, using AI in your sales process feels as natural as using your phone.

Frequently asked questions
Do I have to do the missions in order?

Yes — the missions are sequenced from beginner to advanced. Completing them in order builds on each previous skill.

How much time does each mission take?

10–20 minutes. Designed to fit into your morning routine before calls start.

What if I miss a day?

Just pick up where you left off. The missions don't expire. Consistency matters more than perfection.

Can a sales manager assign these to their team?

Yes — share the page URL with your team and set a daily check-in to discuss each mission. Works well as a team training activity.
