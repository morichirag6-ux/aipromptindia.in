How to Use ChatGPT for B2B Sales in India — Beginner's Guide

ChatGPT is the most powerful free tool available to Indian salespeople right now. But most people either don't know how to use it for sales, or they use it wrong. This guide shows you exactly how to use it for every stage of your B2B sales process — from lead research to closing.

Step 1 — Understand what ChatGPT is good at

ChatGPT is excellent at: writing and rewriting text, research summaries, creating outlines and scripts, generating ideas and alternatives, and explaining complex things simply. It is NOT good at: real-time data, accurate numbers, finding specific contacts, or replacing your judgment on deals.

Step 2 — Set the context every time

The most important rule: always tell ChatGPT who you are and who your customer is before asking anything. Bad prompt: "Write a sales email." Good prompt: "I sell cloud HR software to mid-size IT companies in Hyderabad. My buyer is the HR Head. Write a 100-word cold email focused on saving time on payroll processing."

💡 Pro tip: Use the B2B Lead Builder tool to generate a complete context prompt for your ICP in 60 seconds — then paste that into ChatGPT before every sales task.

Step 3 — Use it for pre-call research

Before any call, paste this: "I have a call with the VP of Sales at [company name], a [industry] company with [size] employees based in [city]. Give me: 3 likely business challenges, 2 recent industry trends, and 5 discovery questions I should ask."

Step 4 — Write cold emails in 2 minutes

Prompt: "Write a cold email to [job title] at [company type] in [city]. My product is [product]. USP is [USP]. Goal of email: get a 15-minute call. Length: 80–100 words. Subject line included. No fluff." Run this 10 times with slight variations and pick the best version.

Step 5 — Prep for objections before your call

Prompt: "I'm calling a CFO about a ₹15 lakh annual software contract. List the top 5 objections a CFO in India would raise and write a 2-sentence response to each." Read these before your call — you'll never be caught off-guard.

Step 6 — Update CRM notes in 30 seconds

After every call, speak your notes into your phone (voice memo), then paste the transcript into ChatGPT: "Clean up these rough call notes into a professional CRM entry. Include: summary, key concerns raised, agreed next steps, and follow-up date." Or use the CRM Notes Generator which does this with one click.

The one habit that changes everything

Create a 'sales context document' — a 300-word description of your product, ICP, value proposition, and top 3 objections. Paste this at the start of every ChatGPT session. This alone will 10x the quality of every output you get.
