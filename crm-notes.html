What is the CRM Notes Generator?

The CRM Notes Generator turns your rough, messy after-call notes into clean, professional CRM entries. Paste your brain dump — even incomplete sentences and shorthand — and get a structured summary with key points, next steps, and follow-up date, ready to paste into HubSpot, Zoho, Pipedrive, or Salesforce.

How to use it — 4 steps
1
Paste your raw notes
Voice memo transcript, rough bullet points, scattered thoughts — paste anything.
2
Select your CRM format
Choose HubSpot, Zoho, Pipedrive, Salesforce, or generic format.
3
Add the call type
Discovery call, demo, negotiation, follow-up, or closing call.
4
Copy the clean entry
Get a structured CRM note ready to paste directly into your CRM system.
Who is this for?

Sales reps, account managers, and SDRs who spend too much time writing CRM notes after calls instead of making more calls. Particularly useful for teams with 5+ calls per day.

Why use an AI prompt instead of doing it manually?

The average salesperson spends 20–30 minutes per day writing CRM notes. That's 2+ hours per week — time that could be spent on selling. This tool cuts note-writing to under 2 minutes per call while producing better, more consistent notes than manual writing.

Frequently asked questions
Does it work with voice memo transcripts?

Yes — paste any transcript or rough notes. Even incomplete sentences and abbreviations work fine.

Which CRMs does it support?

Works with any CRM. The prompt output is formatted for HubSpot, Zoho CRM, Pipedrive, Salesforce, and generic formats.

Will it capture next steps automatically?

Yes — the prompt specifically extracts and highlights next steps and follow-up dates from your notes.

Can I use it for meeting notes (not just calls)?

Absolutely — client meetings, demos, discovery sessions, and any other interaction where you need clean documentation.
