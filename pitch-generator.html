What is the Pitch Generator?

The Pitch Generator creates three versions of your sales pitch simultaneously — a 30-second elevator pitch, a 2-minute phone call pitch, and a written email or LinkedIn pitch. All three are tailored to your specific product, target buyer, and value proposition. No more generic pitches that sound like every other salesperson.

How to use it — 4 steps
1
Describe your product
What it does, who it's for, and the main problem it solves.
2
Define your target buyer
Role, industry, and company size of the person you're pitching to.
3
Enter your top value point
The single biggest benefit or outcome your product delivers.
4
Copy all three pitches
Get 30-second, 2-minute, and written versions in one click.
Who is this for?

Salespeople preparing for a first call, founders pitching their product, SDRs doing cold outreach, and anyone who needs to explain their product clearly and compellingly in a short amount of time.

Why use an AI prompt instead of doing it manually?

Most sales pitches are product-focused — they list features. Buyers don't care about features; they care about outcomes. The Pitch Generator builds outcome-focused pitches that answer the buyer's real question: 'what's in it for me?' Every pitch is specific to the buyer's industry and role, not generic.

Frequently asked questions
Can I generate pitches in different tones?

Yes — select Professional, Friendly, Direct, or Consultative tone in the form. Each produces a distinctly different pitch style.

How do I use the 30-second pitch?

Use it at networking events, at the start of cold calls, when someone asks 'what do you do?' at a conference, or in your LinkedIn headline and summary.

Should I memorise the pitch exactly?

No — use it as a framework. The generated pitch gives you the structure and key points. Deliver it naturally in your own voice.

Can I generate pitches for different buyer types?

Yes — generate one pitch for the CEO, a different one for the IT Manager, another for the CFO. Each needs a completely different angle.
