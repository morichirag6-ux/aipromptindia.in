What is the Sales Objection Handler?

The Sales Objection Handler generates a proven 3-step response script for any sales objection — price, timing, competition, ghosting, authority, and more. Get an Acknowledge → Clarify → Reframe script specifically written for Indian B2B sales situations, ready to use on your next call.

How to use it — 4 steps
1
Select the objection type
Choose from 8 common objection categories — or describe a custom objection.
2
Add your context
Your product, the buyer's role, and deal stage for a tailored response.
3
Copy your script
Get a complete 3-step response script to use on calls or in emails.
4
Practise before the call
Read the script 2-3 times before your call so it sounds natural, not rehearsed.
Who is this for?

Any salesperson who gets objections — which is every salesperson. Particularly useful for SDRs who are newer to handling objections, and experienced salespeople who want scripts for objections they encounter less frequently.

Why use an AI prompt instead of doing it manually?

Most salespeople panic when they hear an objection and either cave immediately (offer a discount) or argue (which kills trust). The 3-step Acknowledge → Clarify → Reframe framework works because it validates the buyer first, which lowers their defenses, then redirects the conversation to value. This is what top performers do instinctively — this tool gives everyone that script.

Frequently asked questions
What are the 8 objection types covered?

Price too high, already using a competitor, not the decision maker, bad timing, need to think about it, no budget, happy with current solution, and no response/ghosting.

Should I use the script word for word?

Use the structure, not the exact words. The script gives you the framework — adapt the specific examples to your product and the buyer's exact situation.

What if the buyer gives a completely different objection?

Describe your custom objection in the text field at the bottom of the tool. The prompt generates a tailored script for any objection.

Does this work for WhatsApp objections?

Yes — the script works for phone calls, emails, and WhatsApp messages. For WhatsApp, shorten the response to 2-3 sentences maximum.
