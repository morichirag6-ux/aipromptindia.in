Best Free AI Tools for Indian Sales Teams in 2026 — No Credit Card Needed

Every week there's a new AI tool promising to transform your sales. Most cost ₹2,000–₹20,000 per month. Here's the truth: you can build a complete AI-powered sales workflow using only free tools. This is the exact stack top Indian sales teams are using in 2026.

1. AI Prompt India (aipromptindia.in) — Free, no login

12 purpose-built AI prompt tools for Indian salespeople. B2B Lead Builder, CRM Notes Generator, Objection Handler, Pitch Generator, Deal Doctor, LinkedIn Boolean Builder, and more. All free, all built for India-specific sales contexts. No account needed.

2. ChatGPT Free Tier — The engine

ChatGPT's free tier (GPT-4o mini) is powerful enough for all sales tasks. Use it to run the prompts from AI Prompt India, write emails, prep for calls, and analyse deals. The paid version (₹1,650/month) is faster but not necessary to start.

💡 Pro tip: The key to getting great results from ChatGPT for free is prompt quality. Use AI Prompt India's tools to generate well-structured prompts — don't just type vague requests.

3. Google Gemini — Free alternative to ChatGPT

Google Gemini is completely free and works well for sales research, email drafting, and summarising long documents. It has better access to current information than ChatGPT's free tier, making it useful for research on companies and industries.

4. Apollo.io Free Tier — Lead database

Apollo.io's free plan gives you 10 email exports per month and unlimited LinkedIn prospecting with their Chrome extension. Use the LinkedIn Boolean Builder to create your search string, then find contacts in Apollo.

5. HubSpot Free CRM — Track your deals

HubSpot's free CRM is the best free option in India. Unlimited contacts, deal tracking, email logging, and a basic sequence tool. After calls, use the CRM Notes Generator to create clean notes to paste into HubSpot.

6. Notion AI (limited free) — Sales knowledge base

Notion's free tier lets you build a sales playbook, store winning prompts, and organise your pipeline. Combine it with the Sales Swipe File to build a personal library of prompts that work for your specific product and market.

The complete free stack

AI Prompt India (prompts) + ChatGPT Free (AI engine) + Apollo Free (leads) + HubSpot Free (CRM) + Gmail (email) = a complete AI-powered sales workflow at ₹0 per month. This stack outperforms paid tools that most companies spend ₹50,000+ per month on.
