AI Prompts for Logistics & Supply Chain Sales in India

India's logistics sector is booming — e-commerce, manufacturing, and FMCG companies are constantly looking for reliable freight and supply chain partners. Here's how logistics sales teams can use AI prompts to win more clients and retain existing ones.

Cold outreach to e-commerce company

Prompt: "I sell third-party logistics (3PL) services in India. Write a cold email to the Operations Head of a D2C e-commerce brand doing ₹5–50 crore in revenue. Focus on last-mile delivery speed and return management. Subject line + 90-word email. No jargon."

Rate negotiation prep

Prompt: "A client wants to renegotiate our logistics contract — they say a competitor is offering 12% lower rates. Help me prepare a counter-argument that focuses on our on-time delivery record, damage rate, and technology integrations. Give me 3 talking points and a closing line."

💡 Pro tip: After any negotiation call, use the CRM Notes Generator to log key discussion points and the agreed rate structure — this prevents disputes later.

Upsell — from spot to contract customer

Prompt: "A customer has been using our spot freight services for 6 months. I want to pitch them a monthly contract for better rates and priority capacity. Write a WhatsApp message to start this conversation. Focus on their benefit (savings + certainty), not our benefit (revenue)."

Handling the 'we have our own fleet' objection

Use the Objection Handler with 'We have our own solution' and customise for logistics. The key argument: own fleet is a fixed cost; outsourcing converts fixed to variable, which protects margins during slow seasons.

Client retention — at-risk account

Prompt: "A key logistics client (₹80 lakh/year) hasn't responded to my last 3 follow-ups. Their contract is up in 60 days. Write a 'business review' email that frames the conversation around their supply chain goals for next year — not our contract renewal."
