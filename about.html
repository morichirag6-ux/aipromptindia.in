👤 About AI Prompt India

Built for Indian sales teams, by someone who gets it

AI Start India is a free collection of AI prompt tools designed to help Indian salespeople, SDRs, founders, and agencies close more deals — without needing a data science degree.

Our mission
Make AI accessible to every Indian seller

Most AI sales tools are built for US enterprise teams with big budgets and dedicated ops teams. Indian salespeople — selling IT services in Pune, real estate in Ahmedabad, financial products in Chennai — don't need enterprise software. They need simple, practical tools that work.

That's exactly what AI Start India (also known as AI Prompt India) is. No jargon, no complex setup, no subscription. Just fill a few fields, click generate, and get a prompt you can use immediately.

🇮🇳
India-first design
Every tool is designed with Indian industries, Indian buyers, and Indian sales culture in mind. From BFSI to IT services to real estate — we get your context.
🆓
Always free, no login
Every tool on AI Start India is free to use. No account, no credit card, no trial. Just open a builder and start generating prompts immediately.

❓ FAQs
Common questions
Who is AI Start India for? +

Built for Indian sales professionals — SDRs, founders, agency owners, real estate agents, BFSI salespeople, and anyone who generates leads or closes deals in India.

Is it really free? +

Yes, completely free. No account, no credit card, no trial period. All 12 tools are free to use forever.

Do I need to know how to use AI? +

No. Fill in a few fields about your product and buyer, click generate, and get a ready-to-use prompt. Paste it into ChatGPT, Gemini, or any AI tool you already use.

Which AI tool does it work with? +

The prompts work with any AI — ChatGPT, Google Gemini, Claude, Perplexity, or any other large language model.

Does AI Start India store my data? +

No. All fields are processed in your browser. We do not collect or store anything you type into the tools.
