Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What we collect

AI Start India does not require you to create an account or log in. We do not collect any personal information to use our prompt builders. If you contact us via the contact form or email, we receive only the information you voluntarily provide (name, email, message).

How we use information

Any information provided through the contact form is used solely to respond to your message. We do not sell, share, or distribute your personal information to any third parties.

Cookies & analytics

We may use basic analytics (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use our site. This data is anonymised and used only to improve our tools. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Prompt data

All prompts are generated locally in your browser. The text you enter into our builders is not stored on our servers, not sent to us, and not shared with any third parties. Your input leaves only when you choose to copy it and paste it into an AI tool of your choice.
