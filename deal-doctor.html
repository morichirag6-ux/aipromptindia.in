What is the Deal Doctor?

The Deal Doctor diagnoses your stuck deal and generates a complete revival plan. Describe the deal — what stage it's at, what happened last, how long it's been stuck — and get a diagnosis of the real problem plus email templates, WhatsApp messages, and call scripts to get it moving again.

How to use it — 4 steps
1
Describe the stuck deal
Company, deal value, stage, and how long it's been silent or stalled.
2
Explain what happened
Last interaction, what was discussed, any signals you noticed.
3
Select deal size & urgency
Small deals need different revival tactics than enterprise deals.
4
Copy your revival kit
Get diagnosis + email + WhatsApp message + call script all in one prompt.
Who is this for?

Sales reps with deals sitting in pipeline for too long, account managers managing renewal risks, and anyone who has a prospect that was interested but has gone quiet or stalled at proposal or negotiation stage.

Why use an AI prompt instead of doing it manually?

Most salespeople either give up on stuck deals too early or keep sending the same follow-up and getting ignored. The Deal Doctor identifies the real reason a deal is stuck — budget freeze, internal politics, lost champion, or lost interest — and gives the right revival approach for each specific situation.

Frequently asked questions
How long does a deal need to be stuck before using this?

Use it when a deal hasn't moved in 7+ days after a clear next step was agreed. Earlier is better.

What if the prospect chose a competitor?

The Deal Doctor includes a 'lost to competitor' scenario with a specific re-engagement approach for 60–90 days later.

Does it work for small deals under ₹1 lakh?

Yes — set the deal size to small and the prompt generates shorter, more direct revival messages appropriate for smaller transactions.

What if I don't know why the deal is stuck?

That's exactly what the Deal Doctor is designed for. Describe what you know and it will identify the most likely reason from the signals you provide.
