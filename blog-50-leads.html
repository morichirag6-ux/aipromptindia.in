How Indian SDRs Use AI Prompts to Generate 50 Leads a Day

50 leads a day sounds like a lot. And manually, it is — it would take 6–8 hours. But with AI, the entire research, personalisation, and outreach drafting workflow takes less than 2 hours. Here's the exact system top Indian SDRs are using.

Step 1 — Build your ICP in 10 minutes (once)

Use the B2B Lead Builder on AI Start India to generate a detailed ICP (Ideal Customer Profile) prompt. Paste it into ChatGPT and save the output. This becomes your North Star for all prospecting — you only need to do this once per campaign.

Step 2 — Find 50 companies in 20 minutes

Use the LinkedIn Boolean Builder to create a precise search string. Search on LinkedIn, Apollo.io, or even Google (site:linkedin.com "job title" "company type" "city"). Copy the company names and decision-maker names into a spreadsheet. You should have 50 rows in 15–20 minutes.

Step 3 — Research 10 high-priority prospects (30 minutes)

Don't research all 50 deeply — it's a waste of time. Pick the top 10 that fit your ICP best. For each, run the B2B Lead Builder prompt in ChatGPT. You'll get company profile, decision-maker insights, and a personalised outreach angle in 2–3 minutes per prospect.

Step 4 — Write and send outreach (45 minutes)

For the top 10, write personalised emails using the research. For the remaining 40, use a semi-personalised template — change the company name, industry, and one specific detail. Tools like Instantly.ai or even Gmail's mail merge can help you send at scale.

Step 5 — Log CRM notes (15 minutes)

At the end of each day, paste your notes into the CRM Notes Generator. It gives you a clean summary and follow-up task for each interaction. Your CRM stays up-to-date without spending an hour on admin.

Total time: ~2 hours for 50 prospects researched, personalised, and contacted. That's what used to take a full working day.
