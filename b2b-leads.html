What is the B2B Lead Builder?

The B2B Lead Builder generates a structured research prompt you paste into ChatGPT or Gemini. In seconds you get company intelligence, decision-maker profiles, pain points, and personalised outreach angles — all tailored to your specific product and target buyer.

How to use it — 4 steps
1
Describe your product
Be specific — 'cloud HR software for mid-size IT companies' gets better results than 'software'.
2
Select your target
Choose company type, size, decision-maker role, and city in India.
3
Pick research depth
Light for quick bullet points, Deep for a full research report.
4
Copy & paste
Copy the generated prompt → open ChatGPT or Gemini → paste → get instant prospect research.
Who is this for?

B2B salespeople, SDRs, account executives, founders doing their own sales, and agency BD teams in India who need to research prospects before outreach calls or emails.

Why use an AI prompt instead of doing it manually?

Manual prospect research takes 30–60 minutes per account. Most salespeople skip it and send generic emails that get ignored. This tool turns research into a 2-minute task — so you always go into every call knowing exactly what matters to that buyer.

Frequently asked questions
Does this work for international prospects?

Yes — just type the city or country in the location field. Works for US, UK, UAE, Singapore, and any other market.

Which AI tool should I paste the prompt into?

ChatGPT (free tier works), Google Gemini, Claude, or Perplexity — all work. ChatGPT gives the most detailed output.

How often should I research a prospect?

Before every first call, before sending a proposal, and after any significant company news (funding, new hire, acquisition).

Can I use this for BFSI or real estate prospects?

Yes — select BFSI or Real Estate in the company type dropdown. The prompt adjusts automatically for sector-specific intelligence.
